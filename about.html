What We Serve!

Why to join Us

Al Musleh foundation School work with the cooperation of Al Musleh Welfare Society, with the staff of highly qualified teachers and cooperative management to enlighten the future of our youth with modern Academic and Islamic education.

Who we are?

Al Musleh Foundation School or AMFS is one of the well known and reputed private owned schools in Karachi, Pakistan. Get here more information about admission, fee structure, contact Number, jobs and campuses details. School serves in two shifts, morning shift and afternoon shift. Morning shift is for Pre-Primary, Primary and Secondary (only girls) section while Afternoon shift is for Secondary boys.

Our Mission

Our mission is to prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, and thus make the world a better and more just place. We will ensure that our students develop both the skills a sound liberal arts education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy. We will also lead in generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities. We aim to develop and groom our students educationally, morally and socially with a team of highly qualified teachers and efficient management.
