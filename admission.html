Our Mission

Our mission is to prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, and thus make the world a better and more just place. We will ensure that our students develop both the skills a sound liberal arts education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy. We will also lead in generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities. We aim to develop and groom our students educationally, morally and socially with a team of highly qualified teachers and efficient management.

Admission Criteria

A student must pass an exam for admission at Al Musleh Foundation School. Admission test is managed by school management to check student eligibility. Registration for admissions starts in March. Thousand of parent try and wish for admission of their child in AMFS but only lucky one can get admission in Al Musleh Foundation School. All the students are admitted only strict merit policy by passing admission test. Any how, you can prepare your child for kgs from their childhood by knowing exact admission criteria. Contact with headmistress to know more about admission fees, criteria, tests, Interview, admission dates and others related queries.
